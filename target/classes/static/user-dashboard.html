About QuizMaster

QuizMaster is a powerful and interactive quiz platform tailored for programming students, tech enthusiasts, and developers preparing for interviews or coding assessments. Our application brings you a seamless experience to test and expand your programming knowledge across multiple languages and technologies like Java, Python, C++, C, JavaScript, SQL, and MongoDB.

Designed with a focus on engagement and fairness, QuizMaster features:

  • 🎯 Smart and dynamic question selection from real-time APIs
  • ⏱️ Fully timed quizzes with intuitive progress tracking
  • 🔒 Anti-cheat monitoring to ensure focused participation
  • 🧠 Instant scoring, performance feedback, and results breakdown
  • 🌍 Start All Quiz mode: Challenge yourself across every topic!

Whether you're revising for technical interviews, brushing up on a specific skill, or just want to challenge your friends — QuizMaster is your go-to companion for becoming a programming pro. Let's sharpen your mind and level up your coding game!
